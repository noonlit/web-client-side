Story and Plot

A story happens because a routine is interrupted. Here are three events that might spark a story:

  1. Jane is living in her studio apartment and happily working as an engineer. One day, she finds a new door beside her bed.
  2. Jane is living in her studio apartment and happily working as an engineer. One day, she receives a phone call from herself.
  3. Jane is living in her studio apartment and happily working as an engineer. One day, she discovers that her upstairs neighbour is a Mesopotamian demon.
